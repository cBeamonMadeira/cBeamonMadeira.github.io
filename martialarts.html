Martial Arts

I started with a picture of white boxing gloves. First, I removed the label on the gloves using the stamp tool. Then, I used the curves adjustment to make the current picture slightly more greenish. Then, I used the lasso tool to select an area of the box. I inverted the selection and used the brush tool to outline the area. I had selected. Then, I inverted the selection again and removed the area in the boxing glove. I imported an image of a galaxy and moved the layer under the layer with the gloves. For the second glove, I downloaded various images of butterflies, used the lasso tool to isolate the butterflies from the rest of the image they came from, placed them on and around the boxing glove, and used the adjustments (specifically levels and curves) to customize their colors. Finally, I added noise to the image.
